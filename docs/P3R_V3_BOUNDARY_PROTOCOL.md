# P3R-v3 Frozen Boundary Extension

P3R-v2 is permanently recorded as a NO-GO and is not reinterpreted. Its grid
showed two pre-specified boundary signals: aggregate validation AUC increased
monotonically through the largest residual weight, and smaller histogram
energy generally preserved the GAP semantic backbone best. P3R-v3 therefore
changes only these two ranges: `gamma` is extended down to 0.005 and the
residual weight up to 1.0. The query, sensitivity proof, frozen per-dataset GAP
backbones, privacy accountant, visible-message adversary, decoder family,
nested selection, and materiality gates are unchanged.

This is explicitly adaptive development on the already used P3 validation
data. A GO cannot support a paper claim by itself. It may only authorize a new
protocol frozen before acquiring fresh confirmatory graph sources. The
encrypted P3 test remains permanently prohibited.
